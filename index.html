Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 15, 2026
Summary: Craftaria does not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. All your game progress is stored only on your own device. The only exception is the optional multiplayer feature, which connects your device directly to another player's device — explained in detail below.

This Privacy Policy describes how Craftaria ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, the App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, device identifiers, or any similar data. We have no servers that store user data, and we have no way to identify individual players.

2. Local Data Storage (On Your Device)

All game data — including your saved worlds, inventory, settings, and progress — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us, and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, this local data is permanently deleted from your device. You are in full control of it at all times.

3. Multiplayer (Peer-to-Peer)

The App includes an optional multiplayer feature. If you choose to host or join a multiplayer session, the App uses a peer-to-peer (P2P) connection so that your device communicates directly with the other player's device to synchronize gameplay (such as block changes, player positions, and world state). We do not operate a game server, and we do not receive, store, or process any of this gameplay data.

To establish a direct peer-to-peer connection, standard WebRTC technology requires the help of intermediary connection servers. These servers are used only momentarily to connect two devices:

  • Signaling server: Used to exchange the technical information two devices need in order to find and connect to each other (for example, network addresses). This happens only while a connection is being set up.
  • STUN/TURN servers: Used to help devices behind routers/firewalls establish a connection. In some network conditions, a TURN relay server may temporarily pass encrypted gameplay traffic between the two devices if a direct connection cannot be made.

During this connection process, your device's IP address may be visible to the other player's device and to these intermediary servers, as is inherent and unavoidable in any direct internet connection between two devices (including video calls, online games, and file sharing). We do not log, collect, or store IP addresses or any connection information. This information is handled transiently by the third-party services solely to establish connectivity.

If you do not use the multiplayer feature, no network connection of this kind is made, and the App functions fully offline.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party technologies for the peer-to-peer multiplayer feature. We do not control these services, and their handling of transient connection data is governed by their own policies:

  • PeerJS / WebRTC — for establishing peer-to-peer connections.
  • STUN/TURN relay servers — for connectivity between devices on restrictive networks.

The App does not include any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or social media trackers.

5. No Advertising, Analytics, or Tracking

The App contains no advertisements. We do not use any analytics or usage-tracking tools, and we do not build user profiles or track your activity across apps or websites.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the App does not collect personal information from anyone, no personal information from children is collected. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, though as described above, we do not have any personal data to remove.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on any server, there is no central store of user data that could be exposed in a breach. Your game data remains solely on your device, protected by your device's own security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since all your data is stored locally and we collect nothing, you have complete control:

  • You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App.
  • You can choose never to use the multiplayer feature, keeping the App fully offline.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
